TUSK Whitepaper

Published on May 12, 2025

Abstract

TUSK is a bonding curve-based token and launchpad ecosystem built on the Sui blockchain, integrated with the Walrus Protocol. It empowers meme coin creators, Web3 communities, and DeFi participants with fair token distribution, sustainable liquidity, and decentralized governance.

Introduction

Bonding curves link token supply and price dynamically. TUSK leverages this for a secure, transparent launchpad, avoiding traditional presales and centralized distributions. Hosted at tusk.wal.app, TUSK democratizes token launches, ensuring liquidity and price discovery align with demand, built for simplicity, trust, and long-term value.

Vision and Mission

Vision: To be the premier bonding curve launchpad for next-generation meme coins and utility tokens on Sui.
Mission: To deliver a seamless, transparent, and equitable token launch process using bonding curves for continuous liquidity and price integrity.

Platform Architecture

Smart Contract Infrastructure

TUSK uses Move smart contracts on Sui for secure, auditable bonding curve logic, native asset support, low fees, and rapid transaction finality.

Key Modules

  • BondingCurve.move: Manages minting and burning logic based on a predefined curve.
  • TokenFactory.move: Issues and oversees creator tokens launched on TUSK.
  • Treasury.move: Handles liquidity collection, allocating a portion to the Walrus Protocol reserve.
  • Governance.move: Enables future DAO upgrades for community voting and participation.

How TUSK Launchpad Works

  • Creator Application: Token creators apply to launch on Tusk.
  • Curve Configuration: Select bonding curve parameters (e.g., linear, exponential).
  • Token Minting: Users bond SUI to mint new tokens.
  • Liquidity Accumulation: A portion of bonded funds contributes to a liquidity pool.
  • Listing Support: Automatic DEX listings (e.g., Cetus, BlueMove).
  • Governance Listing: Community votes on featured launches.

Bonding Curve Models

  • Linear: Price increases at a fixed rate per token minted.
  • Exponential: Price rises exponentially with supply.
  • Sigmoid (Future): Planned for slower initial growth, accelerating mid-curve, then stabilizing.
